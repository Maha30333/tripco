Departure & Arrival

Morning/Evening: Departure from the starting point and travel toward our destination.
Afternoon: Arrival at the destination, hotel check-in, and time to unpack.
Evening: Welcome dinner, brief orientation session with the tour guide, and overnight stay.

Day 02 :
Full-Day Sightseeing & Exploration

Morning: Breakfast at the hotel followed by a departure for prime sightseeing locations.
Afternoon: Guided tour of famous local landmarks, historical spots, and scenic viewpoints. Lunch break at a popular local eatery.
Evening: Free time for shopping, photography, exploring local markets, and dinner.

Adventure, Leisure & Return Journey

Morning: Breakfast at the hotel and checkout.
Afternoon: Short visit to remaining nearby attractions or time for optional recreational activities.
Evening: Departure for the return journey back to the city of origin.
